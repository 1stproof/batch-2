**Notes**
- *Misattributed basis:* Step 6 of the proof casually misattributes the structural basis of the super-coinvariant algebra to Haglund, Rhoades, and Shimozono (2018). That paper actually constructs a *purely commutative* generalized coinvariant ring ($R_{n,k}$), not the anti-commutative super-coinvariant algebra ($SR_n$) developed by Zabrocki. 
- *Broken $d=0$ collapse:* The proof's final note relies on the derivation operator kernel mapping down to positive-degree Mahonian permutations for $d=0$. Cheng & Wang (2012) explicitly shows the kernel of $Q = \sum \theta_i \partial_{x_i}$ on pure polynomials consists strictly of constants, making this proposed continuous-to-discrete collapse mathematically false. Discrete statistics on OSPs (from Haglund, Remmel, Wilson 2018) are required to avoid this continuous operator fallacy.
