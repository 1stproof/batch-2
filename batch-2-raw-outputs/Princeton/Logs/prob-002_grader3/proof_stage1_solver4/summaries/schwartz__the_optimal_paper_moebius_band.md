## Definitions
- **Definition.** A smooth paper Moebius band of aspect ratio λ is an infinitely differentiable isometric mapping I : Mλ → R3 , where Mλ is the flat Mobius band obtained by identifying the length-1 sides of a 1 × λ rectangle. That is: Mλ = ([0, λ] × [0, 1])/ ∼, (0, y) ∼ (λ, 1 − y).
